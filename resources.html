Here are some helpful resources for mental health support:

National Suicide Prevention Lifeline

Call or text: 988

Crisis Text Line

Text HOME to 741741 for free, 24/7 crisis support

National Helpline for Substance Abuse and Mental Health Services

Call: 1-800-662-HELP (1-800-662-4357)
